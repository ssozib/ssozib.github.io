WELCOME TO THE SCHOOL OF YOUR DREAMS
Founded in 1905 by British missionaries, kabimgo comprehensive has a rich history of academic excellence and is one of the oldest and most prestigious schools in Uganda. The school originally started as an institution to educate girls in domestic sciences and Christian values. Over time, it evolved into a comprehensive educational institution focusing on both academics and personal development.
Excellence in National Examinations: kabingo consistently ranks among the top schools in Uganda in the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) and Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. Its students routinely achieve top scores in subjects like mathematics, sciences, and humanities, securing spots in prestigious universities both within and outside Uganda.
Integration of ICT: kabingo has embraced Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in its teaching methods, equipping both students and teachers with the necessary skills to thrive in a modern, technology-driven world. The introduction of e-learning platforms and online resources has greatly improved access to knowledge, helping the school maintain its reputation for academic excellence.
International Competitions: kabingo  students regularly participate in regional and international competitions in areas like debate, robotics, and innovation, further enhancing the school’s academic profile
